Task Description

In the next part of the study, you will play the role of an engineer investigating an unfamiliar machine with four nodes. Three of the nodes are switches you can turn ON or OFF, and the fourth is an outcome light controlled by a hidden rule.

You will run a series of trials. On each trial, you can test the machine as many times as you like before typing out, in your own words, what you think the hidden rule is. There is no right or wrong way to explore — just do what feels natural to you.

The task takes most of the study's time, so please find a moment when you can focus without interruption.
